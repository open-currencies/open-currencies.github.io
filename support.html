How to support the Open Currencies Foundation?

In order to support the OCF and its cause financially please transfer liquidity in any desired amount and in any of the available OCS currencies / obligations to the OCF's donations account with the ID

11125q3eiDkDKudcUAwRIyQVETu

and hash value 78A78BD:1AE566E:38FF2E2:D84DB50:CD9D026:6A1896A:25AEEDE:340B51F.
To be able to make transfers to the above account add it to your client under a suitably chosen local name, e.g. OCF_Donations_Account.

We welcome any support as it will be used to improve existing software, add new features and promote the concepts and ideas the OCS is based on.
